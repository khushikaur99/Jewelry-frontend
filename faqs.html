What can I return?

1. You may request return for an item listed as returnable on the product listing page within the stipulated return window.
2. For details refer to our Return Policy

Can I replace an item?

1. If the item you ordered arrived in a physically damaged or defective condition or is different from their description on the product detail page, or has missing parts or accessories, it will be eligible for a free replacement, provided the exact item is available in stock.
2. In case the same item is not available in stock, we will initiate refund

How do I return/replace an item?

1. In order to return an order, go to “Account” section on OY Jewells website, select “Order History”
2. Login using registered mobile (used to place the order) and OTP received
3. Select the order you wish to return/replace

What are the return guidelines?

1. The item you wish to return must be eligible for return;
2. If return is not eligible for reverse pickup, a self-ship option will be given* (customers can claim refund of up to Rs 100 against reverse shipping charges);
3. Once return is received, in accordance with Return Policy, you will be issued refund to your original mode of payment;
4. For Cash on Delivery orders, refund will be processed to your bank account either via NEFT or UPI.

When will I get my refund?

1. Refund is processed once the returned items are received by OY Jewells or the Seller notifies us of the receipt of the return
2. Timeline for refund to be processed is 7-10 business days form the date of receipt of product by Seller.

What are the guidelines to accept shipments?

1. Before accepting shipment of any product, please ensure that the product’s packaging is not damaged or tampered.
2. In case the package is damaged or tampered, you are requested to refuse to accept delivery. We will assure you refund upon such refused delivery, provided the order is prepaid.
